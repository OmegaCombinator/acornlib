# Uniform Spaces and Metric Spaces

Goal: formalize limits and completeness in a way that scales beyond concrete metric arguments.

- [ ] Introduce filters or a comparable convergence abstraction
- [ ] Build neighborhood-filter and `tendsto` style APIs
- [ ] Define uniform spaces and uniform continuity
- [ ] Add metric, pseudometric, and extended metric spaces
- [ ] Support Cauchy sequences, Cauchy filters, and completeness
- [ ] Construct completions of metric and uniform spaces
- [ ] Add total boundedness and compactness criteria
- [ ] Support Lipschitz maps, isometries, and uniform embeddings
- [ ] Build product and function-space metric constructions
- [ ] Connect uniform-space results cleanly to topological ones
